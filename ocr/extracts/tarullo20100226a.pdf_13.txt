Conclusion 

In closing, let me say that regulatory reform will not come to a close once we 

have enacted our new regulations and legislation.  The work of containing systemic risk 

and the too-big-to-fail problem will need to be adaptive.  The perspectives, ideas, and 

criticisms of those outside the regulatory agencies will remain essential to this work, even 

if they can sometimes cause some discomfort for those of us within.
